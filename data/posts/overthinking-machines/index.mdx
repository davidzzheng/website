Large language models are undoubtedly the consequential technology of our time. In just a few short years since their introduction by OpenAI back in 2022 with ChatGPT, they have engrained themselves so deeply in society that they've become the "second person" that many people rely on, and permeate into every leaf of the proverbial tech tree. What was thought to be just a just another fad in the ever-revolving door of Silicon Valley churns out quickly proved itself to the one to stay.
